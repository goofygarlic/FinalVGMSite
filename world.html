Composer Profiles

Toby Fox

Location: Boston, MA, USA

Signature Track: “Megalovania”

Era: 2015–Present

Toby Fox’s driving, glitchy anthem that became the defining boss theme of Undertale.

Shoji Meguro

Location: Tokyo, Japan

Signature Track: “Life Will Change”

Era: 1995–Present

Meguro’s jazzy, funky rebellion anthem from Persona 5 that captures youthful defiance.

Nobuo Uematsu

Location: Kōchi, Japan

Signature Track: “One-Winged Angel”

Era: 1986–Present

Uematsu’s epic fusion of orchestra and choir from Final Fantasy VII’s climactic showdown.

Junichi Masuda

Location: Tokyo, Japan

Signature Track: “Route 1 Theme”

Era: 1996–Present

Masuda’s upbeat chiptune melody that greets trainers on their first steps in Pokémon Red/Blue.

Yasunori Mitsuda

Location: Shūnan, Japan

Signature Track: “Corridors of Time”

Era: 1992–Present

Mitsuda’s haunting blend of synth and percussion that defines Chrono Trigger’s time-travel.

C418

Location: Berlin, Germany

Signature Track: “Sweden”

Era: 2006–Present

Daniel “C418” Rosenfeld’s serene piano loop that greets players in Minecraft’s early hours.

Christopher Larkin

Location: Sydney, Australia

Signature Track: “Greenpath”

Era: 2017–Present

Larkin’s lush harp and strings that accompany exploration in Hollow Knight’s verdant realm.

Motoi Sakuraba

Location: Osaka, Japan

Signature Track: “Beyond the Sky”

Era: 1985–Present

Sakuraba’s soaring guitar and percussion that close out Tales of Symphonia’s grand finale.

Jeremy Soule

Location: Seattle, WA, USA

Signature Track: “Dragonborn”

Era: 1994–Present

Soule’s powerful Nordic horn fanfare that has become Skyrim’s unmistakable theme.
